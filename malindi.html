Mombasa, Kenya

Sarova Whitesands Beach Resort & Spa

Share

East Africa’s finest resort, Whitesands Beach Resort & Spa is conveniently located on one of the longest stretches of magnificent beach fronts on the Kenyan coast.

Guests enjoy a most relaxing and comfortable stay in tastefully furnished rooms and suites offering spectacular ocean, pool, and garden views. For dining and entertainment, guests can choose from three superb restaurants & two great bars, overlooking the serene Indian Ocean.

Swimming Pools

Our 5 swimming pools include a 'silent' pool dedicated to those who just need to wind down by the pool with a book, a pool with a bar and one with a slide.

 

Tulia Wellness Spa

Our Spa offers Ayurvedic, Western and Oriental treatments. The Ayurvedic treatments are based on an ancient Indian form of holistic healing where health is a result of harmony in body, mind and spirit.

 

Tulia Fitness Center

Exercise equipment includes cross trainers, exercise cycles, free weights, multi gym, rowing machines and treadmills.

 

Ozone Kids Club

Children love the Sarova Whitesands Beach Resort & Spa, a popular getaway destination on the Northern coast for families. A wide variety of in and out-door activities are planned and the property has its very own children's playground.
